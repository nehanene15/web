About Me:

I am a junior at San Diego State University, majoring in Computer Science and double minoring in Mathematics and Interdisciplinary Studies. My passion lies in the overlap of technology, math, and design. I am very interested in the future of technology, especially regarding virtual reality and artificial intelligence. I have experience with data analysis, computer programming in Java and Python, and front-end web develompement. I love to travel and explore nature, but I am also drawn to the fast-paced atmosphere of big cities.

My Experience ↓

  • 2011 Started my first job as a math tutor for elementary and middle school children at the Pleasanton Public Library.
  • 2012 Competed in DECA competitions and placed in the top 20 in the world for my event. It involved formulating a business plan given a time limit of 10 minutes and presenting it to the judge in a roleplay situation. It served as a valuable introduction to professionalism, time management, and multitasking.
  • 2013 Interned at Galileo Innovation Summer Camp, where I helped instructors in classrooms and organized events for the students. I learned the value of hard work and persistence.
  • 2014 Member of ACE Coding Club , learning the basics of Java and computer systems.
  • 2015 Started at San Diego State University as a member of the Weber Honors College.
  • 2016 Interned at PayRange, analyzing big data using Tableau, Flurry, and Excel. I presented my findings in bi-weekly powerpoints via Zoom to the entire company. This was my rite of passage into a fast-paced, challenging startup environment. I also experimented with Arduino and learned Python.
  • 2016 FALL Began working as a student assistant at the Instructional Technology Services at San Diego State University. I assist faculty with Blackboard, Parscore, and any other technological issues. I get to experiment with virtual reality using HTC Vive and new educational technology such as Learning Glass.
  • 2017 Selected as Vice President of Academics for the Weber Honors College. I will be organizing events such as LinkedIn photoshoots and professional development workshops. I am currently working on a sign in system for members of the college to automize taking attendance at our many events.
  • 2017 SUMMER Aside from my web development projects, this summer I will be interning in Madrid, Spain at Plentie, an app company, as a computer programmer. I will be working on creating functional designs to increase performance marketing and learning back end development with PHP and SQL.
